Stefano (Pio) Zingaro is a Junior Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the [Department of Computer Science and Engineering](//www.cse.unibo.it/en), [University of Bologna](//www.unibo.it/en). 

He is also part of the research team [FOCUS](//team.inria.fr/focus/) at [Inria Sophia Antipolis-Méditerranée](//www.inria.fr/fr/centre-inria-sophia-antipolis-mediterranee).

He specializes in **programming languages**, **artificial intelligence**, and **computer science education**.

### Erdös Number

Stefano has an Erdös number of **4** (via [Martini](//www.cs.unibo.it/~martini/index.html) → [Scedrov](//www.cis.upenn.edu/~scedrov/) → [Blass](//www.math.lsa.umich.edu/~ablass/) → [Erdös](//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Erd%C5%91s)).

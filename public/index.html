Stefano (Pio) Zingaro is a Junior Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Bologna.

He is also part of the research team FOCUS at Inria Sophia Antipolis-Méditerranée.

He specializes in programming languages, artificial intelligence, and computer science education.

Erdös Number

Stefano has an Erdös number of 4 (via Martini → Scedrov → Blass → Erdös).
